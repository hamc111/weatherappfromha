Recommended Drink: Blue Hawaii

Why don't you try pretty cocktail just like
the beautiful sunny weather?

Ingredients

  • 3/4 ounce vodka
  • 3/4 ounce light rum
  • 1/2 ounce blue curacao
  • 3 ounces pineapple juice
  • 1 ounce sweet-and-sour mix
  • Garnish: pineapple wedge

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
    (Or blend all ingredients with ice in a blender.)
  2. Strain into a glass over pebble ice.
    (Or pour from blender into glass with no ice.)
