Recommended Food: Club Sandwich

Easy food is the best on rainy days when it is difficult to cook!

Ingredients

  • 4 rashers streaky bacon
  • 3 slices white bread
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 hard-boiled egg, sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • few thick slices chicken breast
  • small handful salad leaves
  • handful crisps, to serve, if you like

Steps

  1. Heat grill to High and cook the bacon, turning halfway through, until crisp. Toast the bread and spread one slice with the mayo.
  2. Layer on the egg, tomato and bacon, then top with another slice of bread. Add the chicken or turkey, bacon, then lettuce. Finish with the final slice of bread and cut into quarters. Secure with cocktail sticks and serve with crisps, if you like.
