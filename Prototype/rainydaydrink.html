Recommended Drink: Vodka Martini

We recommend the King of Cocktails,
suitable for a rainy night.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces premium vodka
  • 1/2 to 3/4 ounce dry vermouth
  • Optional: 2 dashes bitters
  • Garnish: lemon twist or 3 olives

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Pour the ingredients into a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  3. Shake well or stir for at least 30 seconds.
  4. Strain into a chilled glass.
  5. Garnish with a lemon twist or olives. Serve and enjoy!
